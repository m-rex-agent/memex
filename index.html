memex helm
local-first · loopback-only

Your notes. Your machine.
A console to govern both.

memex runs a vault server on 127.0.0.1 and gives you the helm — files, sync, agent policy, audit. No telemetry, no account, nothing tracked. Notes leave your device only through a cloud remote you set up yourself.

$ memex-helm ✓ server connected transport=http host=127.0.0.1 policy=enforced
